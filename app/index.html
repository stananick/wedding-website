Our Story

Chris and Lindsey met online, but soon realized they had a mutual friend. After talking for about a month, they decided to meet up at the Hofbrauhaus in Pittsburgh, PA (being the beer lovers that they are). They had such a good time on their first date that they ended up staying out until 2:30am on a weeknight!

Fast-forward to December 2015, where Chris and Lindsey went on a vacation to Florida for Christmas with Chris's family. One night, Chris took Lindsey out for some pre-dinner drinks. As the sun was setting, Chris asked his brother to take some photos of him and Lindsey on the beach, which seemed out of character for Chris but Lindsey went along with it. Then, as Chris's brother was taking photos of the two, Chris got down on one knee and asked Lindsey to marry him, and the rest is history!
